Our Mouthguards

At Snugfit, we believe protection shouldn’t come at the cost of comfort. Our custom-made mouth guards are crafted from premium Ethyl Vinyl Acetate (EVA) — a high-quality, latex-free material known for its durability and safety. Each guard offers superior protection, a comfortable fit, and allows for easier breathing during sports. Whether you’re on the field, court, or ring, our mouth guards give you peace of mind at an unbeatable price.

Who Are We?

Snugfit Mouth Guards is your trusted partner in high-quality, customised sports mouth guards at affordable prices. We work closely with schools and clubs, arranging convenient on-site visits to take impressions. For those who prefer a private setting, we also offer impression appointments at our dental rooms. Our goal is to make the process simple, accessible, and tailored to your needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: protect teeth, prevent serious oral injuries, and reduce the risk of concussion often caused when the lower jaw impacts the upper jaw without proper cushioning. We are committed to combining exceptional quality with affordability, making sure athletes of all ages can play with confidence.
